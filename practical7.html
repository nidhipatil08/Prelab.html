Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Missile Man of India

11th President of India

About Him

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was one of India's most respected scientists and leaders. He played a major role in developing India's missile and nuclear programs. He served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007 and was known as the "People's President".

Achievements

He contributed to India's space research and defense systems. He received many awards including the Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian award. Dr. Kalam inspired millions of students with his speeches and books.
